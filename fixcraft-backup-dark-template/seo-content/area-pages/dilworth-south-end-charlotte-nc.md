# Furniture Assembly & Handyman Services in Dilworth, Charlotte NC

Dilworth is one of Charlotte's most beloved neighborhoods — walkable streets, Craftsman bungalows, and a mix of families, young professionals, and long-time residents who love this community.

It's also a neighborhood where furniture assembly has unique challenges. Dilworth homes are old enough to have character — and old enough to have walls that aren't standard drywall.

## Dilworth Homes: What We Work With

### Craftsman Bungalows (1910–1940s)
The backbone of Dilworth. These homes have plaster walls, narrow doorways (that 72" KALLAX requires creative maneuvering), original hardwood floors that we protect religiously, and staircases that weren't designed for queen bed frames.

We've moved and assembled furniture in dozens of Dilworth bungalows. We know how to navigate tight spaces without damage.

### Mid-Century Renovations
Dilworth's post-war homes often mix original architecture with modern interiors. New drywall sections next to original plaster — we identify which is which before drilling.

### Modern Infill and Townhomes
Dilworth's more recent townhome developments (especially along East Blvd and Kenilworth) are standard modern construction — straightforward for assembly, with open floor plans that benefit from strategic furniture placement.
