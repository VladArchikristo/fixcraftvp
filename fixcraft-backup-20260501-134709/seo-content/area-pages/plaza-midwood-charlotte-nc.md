### Vintage & Antique Furniture
We restore and assemble vintage pieces too — loose joints, missing hardware, refinishing prep. If you found something at the Sleepy Poet Antique Mall or a yard sale, we can get it functional.

### TV Mounting in Plaza Midwood
Older homes mean older walls. We work with:
- **Plaster over lath** — requires different anchors and techniques than drywall
- **Exposed brick accent walls** — masonry drilling with hammer drill
- **Exterior-facing walls** with insulation cavities
- **Sloped ceilings** in attic conversions

We assess the wall before drilling. No guesswork.

### Home Studio & Creative Space Setup
Plaza Midwood has Charlotte's highest concentration of musicians, artists, and creatives. We've set up recording studios, art studios, and workshop spaces — shelving, workbenches, cable management, mounting.
