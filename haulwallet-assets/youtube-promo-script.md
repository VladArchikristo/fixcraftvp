# HaulWallet — YouTube Promo Script (60–90 sec)

---

## MUSIC DIRECTION
**Track type:** Cinematic hip-hop / driving anthem — think "dark trap meets industrial"  
**Feel:** Powerful, confident, forward motion. No cheesy country.  
**Reference mood:** Kanye "Power" vibe but trucking-themed.  
**Volume:** Starts low under voiceover, swells on demo sequence, peaks at CTA.

---

## SCENE-BY-SCENE BREAKDOWN

---

### SCENE 1 — HOOK (0–5 sec)

**VIDEO:**  
Close-up of a trucker's hands — crumpled papers, sticky notes, a worn-out notebook on a truck dashboard. Coffee cup tipped over. Phone with 17 unread texts. Windshield shows a dark highway at 3 AM. Hands rubbing tired eyes.

**VOICEOVER:**  
> *"You drive half a million miles a year. But you still manage your business with sticky notes."*

**ON-SCREEN TEXT OVERLAY:**  
```
STILL DOING THIS IN 2024?
```
*(white text, gritty bold font, appears at 3 sec)*

**CUT:** Hard cut to black. 0.3 sec.

---

### SCENE 2 — PROBLEM (5–15 sec)

**VIDEO:**  
Split-screen chaos sequence — fast cuts, 1 sec each:
1. Trucker on phone arguing about a missed load confirmation
2. Spreadsheet nightmare — columns everywhere, manual math
3. Paper receipts falling on the truck floor
4. Late payment email notification on a phone
5. Driver looking stressed staring at fuel receipts

**VOICEOVER:**  
> *"Lost loads. Missed payments. Zero visibility into what you actually earned this week. That's not a business — that's survival mode."*

**ON-SCREEN TEXT OVERLAYS (fast flashes):**
```
LOST LOADS
MISSED PAYMENTS  
NO EARNINGS TRACKING
```
*(red text, each appears then glitches out)*

**AUDIO:** Beat drops heavy on word "survival mode"

---

### SCENE 3 — SOLUTION / DEMO (15–45 sec)

**VIDEO:**  
Smooth, clean reveal. Phone held in trucker's hand — big rig cab background, golden hour light.

**Sub-scene A (15–22 sec) — DASHBOARD**  
Screen shows HaulWallet app dashboard. Finger taps. Numbers update.  
*Animated: earnings counter sweeps up to $4,280 for the week.*

**VOICEOVER:**  
> *"Meet HaulWallet. Everything you need — in one place."*

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:**  
```
HAUL WALLET
All your loads. All your money. One screen.
```

---

**Sub-scene B (22–30 sec) — LOADS TRACKING**  
App screen shows active loads list: pickup addresses, delivery status, timestamps.  
Driver taps a load — full details, documents, broker contact.

**VOICEOVER:**  
> *"Track every load from pickup to delivery. Documents, contacts, status — right here."*

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:**  
```
📦 TRACK EVERY LOAD
Origin → Destination → Delivered ✓
```

---

**Sub-scene C (30–38 sec) — MAP / NAVIGATION**  
Map view with route overlay. Live position dot moving along highway. ETA calculated.

**VOICEOVER:**  
> *"Your route. Your ETA. Your next stop — always visible."*

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:**  
```
🗺️ SMART ROUTING
Know exactly where you are and what's next
```

---

**Sub-scene D (38–45 sec) — EARNINGS DASHBOARD**  
Earnings screen: weekly/monthly chart bars rising. Expense tracker. Net profit number — large, glowing orange.

**VOICEOVER:**  
> *"See your real earnings after fuel, fees, and expenses. Know your numbers like a pro."*

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:**  
```
💰 REAL EARNINGS
After fuel. After fees. Real profit.
```

---

### SCENE 4 — SOCIAL PROOF (45–60 sec)

**VIDEO:**  
Montage of diverse truckers — different rigs, different states. Each holding phone with HaulWallet open. Smiling, confident. One shot: trucker showing app to another trucker at a truck stop. 

*Optional: brief animated counter rolling up: "12,000 truckers and counting"*

**VOICEOVER:**  
> *"Over 12,000 truckers already switched to HaulWallet. They're not going back to the sticky notes."*

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:**  
```
12,000+ TRUCKERS
ALREADY RUNNING SMARTER
```
*(animated counter rolling up from 11,800 to 12,000+, orange text)*

**Testimonial flash (optional, 3 sec):**
```
"I know exactly what I made every single week."
— Marcus T., Owner-Operator, 8 years
```

---

### SCENE 5 — CTA (60–90 sec)

**VIDEO:**  
Wide shot: big rig rolling down an open highway at sunrise. Powerful, free, forward motion. Then cut to clean app store screen on phone. Google Play badge. Hand reaching to tap "Install."

**VOICEOVER:**  
> *"HaulWallet is free to download. No spreadsheets. No chaos. Just your business — finally under control. Download now on Google Play."*

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:**  
```
FREE TO DOWNLOAD
```
*(large, white, center screen — 2 sec)*

```
HaulWallet
```
*(brand name, massive, orange, full screen — 2 sec)*

```
📲 GET IT ON GOOGLE PLAY
haul-wallet.app
```
*(final frame, static hold 3 sec)*

**AUDIO:** Music peaks and fades to clean outro beat. Ends on a single deep truck horn honk (1 sec).

## PRODUCTION NOTES

| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Total duration | 75–80 sec (ideal) |
| Aspect ratio | 16:9 (YouTube standard) |
| Resolution | 4K or 1080p |
| Text font | Bold sans-serif (e.g. Barlow Condensed ExtraBold, or Industry) |
| Primary color | #FF6B00 (orange) |
| Secondary color | #FFFFFF, #0A0A0A |
| VO talent | Male, mid-30s, neutral American accent — sounds like a trucker who got smart, not a corporate narrator |
| Subtitles | Add burnt-in captions for mobile (85% watch without sound) |
| End card | 20-sec YouTube end card with subscribe + app link |
