## Why IKEA Furniture Looks Easy But Isn't

IKEA's design philosophy is brilliant: flat-pack furniture that theoretically anyone can build. In practice, that's only true if you have:

- **The right tools** (not just the included Allen key)
- **3+ uninterrupted hours** per piece
- **Experience reading Swedish-style pictogram instructions**
- **A second pair of hands** for larger pieces
- **Zero pets or kids** in the room while you work

Most Charlotte residents have exactly none of these conditions on a weeknight. And the stakes are real: a warped KALLAX, a PAX wardrobe that isn't level, or cam locks overtightened and stripped can turn a $300 bookcase into a $300 problem.
