# Furniture Assembly & Handyman Services in Myers Park, Charlotte NC

Myers Park is one of Charlotte's most historic and architecturally significant neighborhoods — a tree-lined gem of early 20th-century homes, thoughtful renovations, and long-established families. It's also one of the most unique places to do furniture assembly in the city.

## What Makes Myers Park Different (And Why Experience Matters)

### Plaster Over Lath Walls
Homes built before 1960 — the majority in Myers Park — were constructed with plaster over wooden lath strips, not drywall. Standard stud finders often misread plaster. Wall anchors behave differently. Drilling through plaster without cracking it requires technique.

FixCraft VP has done hundreds of mounts and installations in Myers Park and older Charlotte neighborhoods. We know how to work with plaster respectfully.

### Heritage Floors and Millwork
Original hardwood floors and period millwork define Myers Park homes. We use furniture sliders and floor protection on every job — nothing gets dragged, nothing gets scratched.

### Space Planning Challenges
Pre-war floor plans weren't designed for 86-inch TVs or California King beds. We'll assess your space before assembly and flag any fit issues before you've got a half-assembled wardrobe stuck in a hallway.
