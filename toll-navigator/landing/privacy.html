Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

HaulWallet ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. By using our app or website, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated by your use of the app:

  • Account data — name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Location data — GPS coordinates for toll calculation and IFTA mileage tracking (only when app is active or background tracking is enabled)
  • Trip & route data — routes driven, states crossed, distances traveled
  • Financial data — toll costs, fuel expenses, receipts you scan (stored securely on our servers)
  • Device data — device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs
  • Usage data — features used, session duration, app performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data solely to provide and improve HaulWallet services:

  • Calculate toll costs across 96 toll roads in 20+ US states
  • Generate IFTA mileage reports for fuel tax filing
  • Process and store scanned fuel receipts via OCR
  • Track trip history and expense summaries
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Send service notifications (optional, you can opt out)
  • Improve app performance based on anonymized usage analytics

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data only in these cases:

  • Service providers — hosting (Hetzner), cloud infrastructure used to run our backend servers. They process data only on our behalf under confidentiality agreements.
  • Mapping services — anonymized route data may be sent to mapping APIs (Google Maps) solely for route calculation
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Business transfer — in case of merger or acquisition, your data may transfer to the new owner under the same privacy protections

4. Location Data

Location is the core of IFTA mileage tracking. Here's how we handle it:

  • Foreground location: used only when you actively use toll or trip features
  • Background location: used only when you explicitly enable IFTA background tracking
  • You can disable location access at any time in your device settings
  • Location data is stored on our servers and never sold or shared with advertisers

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers (Hetzner, EU) with:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt — never stored in plain text
  • JWT tokens for secure session authentication
  • Regular security audits and server updates

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us (see below). We will delete your account and associated data within 30 days of your request, except where required to retain it by law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — update inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion — request removal of your account and data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

HaulWallet is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
