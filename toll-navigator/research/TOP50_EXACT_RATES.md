## Notes
- **Dynamic pricing** (I-66, I-95, CA Express Lanes): rates change by demand. Use average off-peak for estimation.
- **Bridge/tunnel fees** are flat, not per mile. Separate from per-mile toll calc.
- **Truck rates** are typically 2–4x passenger rates. This table focuses on 2-axle (passenger/light truck) baseline.
- **2026 increases**: PA, OH, OK, IL, NJ confirmed increases effective Jan-July 2026.
