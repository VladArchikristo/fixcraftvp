# YouTube Upload Guide for HaulWallet

## Video Requirements (YouTube)
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (1080p minimum), 4K preferred
- Duration: 30 sec (short promo) or 75–80 sec (full promo)
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- File size: under 256GB
- Subtitles: burnt-in captions recommended (85% watch without sound)

---

## Quick Production Options

### Option 1: DIY with CapCut / DaVinci Resolve (Free)
1. Record screen demos of the app using iPhone/Android screen recording
2. Add the SVG/PNG assets from `haulwallet-png/` as overlay graphics
3. Use `youtube-thumbnail.svg.png` as the thumbnail
4. Add text overlays in the font: **Barlow Condensed ExtraBold** (free on Google Fonts)
5. Music: [Epidemic Sound](https://www.epidemicsound.com) — search "Confident Drive" or "Cinematic Hip-Hop"
   - Alternative: [Artlist.io](https://artlist.io), [Musicbed.com](https://musicbed.com)
   - BPM target: 85–95
6. Export: H.264, 1080p or 4K, MP4

### Option 2: Screen Recording + Voiceover (Fastest, ~1 day)
1. Record app screens on phone (or use existing PNG mockups as slides)
2. Record voiceover separately — see script below
3. Assemble in iMovie (free on Mac) or CapCut
4. Add text overlays from the script
5. Total cost: $0 (or $15–30 for music license)

### Option 3: Professional (Best quality, $200–800)
- Hire on Fiverr: search "app promo video" or "UGC trucker video"
- Give them: this script, the PNG assets, brand colors (#FF6B00 orange, #FFFFFF, #0A0A0A)
- Delivery: 3–7 days
- Best for paid ad campaigns where quality matters

**Voiceover style:** Male, mid-30s, neutral American accent. Sounds like a trucker who got smart, not a corporate narrator.
