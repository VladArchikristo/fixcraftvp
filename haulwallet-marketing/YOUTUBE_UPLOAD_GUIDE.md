## SEO Tips — Truck Driver Audience

### Best Time to Post

Target when owner-operators are NOT driving. They check phones:
- **Tuesday–Thursday: 6:00–8:00 AM EST** — pre-dispatch, parked at truck stops
- **Sunday: 7:00–9:00 AM EST** — planning the week, fueling up
- **Friday: 5:00–7:00 PM EST** — end of week, loads delivered

Avoid Monday mornings (too busy getting on the road) and Saturday evenings.

**Single best slot: Tuesday 7:00 AM EST**

---

### First 24 Hours — Engagement Actions

The first 24 hours determine how YouTube ranks the video long-term. Do all of this within 2 hours of publishing:

1. **Share immediately in:**
   - Facebook groups: "Owner Operators Only", "Trucking Business Owners", "CDL Truckers USA" (search for active groups with 10k+ members)
   - Reddit: r/Truckers, r/TruckDrivers, r/OwnerOperators — share as honest "I built this, feedback welcome"
   - TikTok — post the vertical cut simultaneously
   - Instagram Reels — same day

2. **Pin a comment** on your own video within 5 minutes of publishing. Example:
   ```
   Download HaulWallet free → haulwallet.app
   Drop a comment if you're an owner-operator — curious what features you need most.
   ```

3. **Reply to every comment in the first 24 hours** — engagement signals matter more than likes.

4. **Ask 5–10 beta users** to watch, like, and comment the day of launch. Real engagement, not fake. Just ask them directly.

5. **Do NOT buy views or subscribers** — YouTube detects velocity anomalies and will suppress the video.
