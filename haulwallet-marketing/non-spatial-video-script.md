# HaulWallet — 30-Second Promo Video Script
## "Stop Losing Money on the Road"
### Format: 16:9 Horizontal | Duration: 30 seconds

---

## CONCEPT

Fast, honest, punchy. Show the problem drivers FEEL every day, then show HaulWallet fixing it. No stock footage of smiling office workers. Real trucks, real roads, real cash.

**Tone:** Gritty + empowering. Like a guy talking to a buddy in a diner.
**Music:** Low, building hip-hop/trap instrumental (think: Kendrick Lamar instrumental energy, not EDM). Beat drops at the solution reveal.

---

## SCENE BREAKDOWN

---

### [0:00 — 0:04] HOOK — THE CHAOS

**Shot:** Close-up. Dashboard covered in crumpled papers, sticky notes, a phone with 7 missed calls, a coffee-stained invoice. Hands frantically sorting through it all.

**Sound:** Stressed music sting. Paper shuffling. Phone buzzing.

**Text overlay (white, bold, center):**
> _"Every load. Every mile. Every headache."_

**Cut to:** Quick flash of a phone calculator with a low number. $320 earned after expenses. Frustration.

---

### [0:04 — 0:08] PROBLEM #1 — LOST MONEY

**Shot:** Driver pulling over on highway shoulder. Scrolling through broker emails on phone. Confused look. Rate confirmation vs. actual payout don't match.

**Sound:** Music tension builds.

**Text overlay (red, sharp):**
> _"Brokers lowballing you?"_
> _"Paperwork eating your time?"_

---

### [0:08 — 0:12] PROBLEM #2 — NO VISIBILITY

**Shot:** Wide angle. Truck parked at a rest stop, 11 PM. Driver eating fast food alone. Laptop open — spreadsheet with manual mileage entries. Tired eyes.

**Sound:** Beat still building.

**Text overlay:**
> _"Running your business from sticky notes?"_

---

### [0:12 — 0:16] THE TURN — MEET HAUWALLT

**Shot:** Phone screen SLAM onto the dash (satisfying). App opens. HaulWallet logo animates in. Clean, fast, confident.

**Sound:** BEAT DROPS. Music shifts — confident, driving rhythm.

**Text overlay (green, large):**
> **HaulWallet.**
> _"Everything in one place."_

---

### [0:16 — 0:20] SOLUTION — THE APP IN ACTION

**Shot:** Screen recording montage, 3 cuts:

1. Load board — two loads side by side, rates visible, one tap to accept. (1.5 sec)
2. Cargo tracker — map view, truck moving, ETA updating, green status. (1.5 sec)
3. Payment screen — $810 earned, invoice auto-generated, FastPay toggle ON. (1 sec)

**Sound:** Three satisfying UI tap sounds synced to each cut.

**Text overlay:**
> _"Find loads. Track cargo. Get paid fast."_

---

### [0:20 — 0:25] RESULT — THE DRIVER WINS

**Shot:** Same driver from the diner scene. Now: phone in one hand, coffee in the other. Leaning back. Load complete. Screen shows: **$1,240 this week**.

**Sound:** Music peaks — triumphant but understated.

**Text overlay (white on dark):**
> _"Mike made $1,240 this week."_
> _"No broker drama. No lost invoices."_

**Quick cut:** Truck rolling down open highway at sunrise. Freedom shot.

---

### [0:25 — 0:30] CTA — DOWNLOAD NOW

**Shot:** App icon, clean background. Then split: App Store / Google Play badges.

**Sound:** Music fades to clean finish.

**Text overlay (full screen):**

```
HAUWALLT

Your Road, Your Rules.

[Download Free]
haulwallet.app
```

**Voiceover (optional, if used):**
> _"HaulWallet. Free to start. Built for the road."_

## MUSIC BRIEF

- **Genre:** Hip-hop instrumental / cinematic trap
- **BPM:** 85–95
- **Structure:**
  - 0:00–0:12: Low, tense, building
  - 0:12–0:16: Beat drop (the turn)
  - 0:16–0:25: Full confident rhythm
  - 0:25–0:30: Clean resolution, fade
- **Reference tracks:** Metro Boomin instrumental vibe, but not aggressive — confident
- **Licensing options:** Epidemic Sound "Confident Drive" category, Artlist, or Musicbed

---

## CAPTION / SUBTITLE VERSION (for social without sound)

Every scene needs text overlays readable without audio. All overlays already included above. Ensure:
- Font: Bold sans-serif (Inter Bold or similar), white with subtle black drop shadow
- Position: Lower third for problem scenes, center for solution/CTA
- Subtitles on by default in social exports
