# HaulWallet — Video Production Brief
## For Agency / Freelance Production Team

---

## PROJECT OVERVIEW

**Product:** HaulWallet — mobile app for trucking owner-operators
**Deliverables:** 30-second promo video (non-spatial) + 60-second XR experience (spatial)
**Use:** Paid social, YouTube, App Store preview, Apple Vision Pro / Android XR marketplace

---

## TARGET AUDIENCE

### Primary: Owner-Operators

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 28–55 |
| Gender | 80% male |
| Location | USA (South, Midwest, Southeast primary markets) |
| Experience | 3–20 years driving, 1–10 years as owner-operator |
| Tech comfort | Moderate — uses phone daily, not early adopter |
| Income | $60K–$120K gross, $40K–$80K net after expenses |
| Pain points | Broker disputes, late payments, lost paperwork, mileage tracking |
| Where they hang out | YouTube (trucking channels), Facebook trucker groups, TikTok (yes, really), Reddit r/Truckers |

### Secondary: Small Fleet Owners (2–5 trucks)
- Same demographics but also care about fleet visibility and driver accountability

### Who this is NOT for:
- Company drivers (they don't own the business decisions)
- Large fleet operators (they have enterprise software)
- Non-trucking logistics (out of scope)

---

## TONE & STYLE

### What it IS:
- **Real.** Not polished corporate. Gritty roads, real trucks, real people.
- **Honest.** Show actual UI. No fake inflated numbers. Show $723 net, not $1,500 gross.
- **Respectful.** These people work hard. Don't be condescending or "app bro" energy.
- **Confident.** The product works. Show it simply. Let it speak.
- **Empowering.** The driver is in control. HaulWallet is the tool, not the hero.

### What it is NOT:
- Corporate stock footage (no smiling diversity-hire office workers)
- Over-produced with unnecessary effects
- Salesy / pushy / fake urgency ("ACT NOW!")
- Overly technical / feature-list focused

### Visual References:
- Ram Trucks commercials (rough, honest, built-tough energy)
- Robinhood early ads (empowerment of regular people vs. the system)
- Calm app video (clean UI showcase without being boring)

### Color Palette (app brand):
- Primary: Deep navy `#0A1628`
- Accent: Electric green `#00D68F`
- White and light gray for text
- Amber/gold for earnings highlights `#FFB347`

---

## KEY MESSAGES

### Message 1: Control
> "You work for yourself. Your tools should work for you."

Drivers chose owner-operator life for independence. HaulWallet gives them the business infrastructure to actually run their business — not just drive.

### Message 2: Clarity
> "Know what you're making before you move a wheel."

No more load acceptance guesswork. Real rates, real expenses, real net pay — upfront, always.

### Message 3: Speed
> "Less paperwork. Faster payment. More road."

Every minute on admin is a minute not earning. HaulWallet compresses hours of paperwork into seconds, and gets money moving faster.

## BUDGET OPTIONS

### Option A: DIY (Under $500)
**Best for:** Getting first content live, testing messaging before investing more

**What you need:**
- iPhone 14 Pro or newer (4K 60fps capable) — already owned
- A real truck (borrow from a driver who uses the app)
- DJI OM 6 or similar stabilizer: ~$120
- Epidemic Sound / Artlist license for music: $15/month or $200/year
- CapCut Pro or DaVinci Resolve (free): $0–$10/month
- Screen recording of the app with iOS built-in: free
- Natural lighting only (golden hour — free)

**Realistic output:** 30s promo, vertical + landscape. Honest and authentic feel. Works well on TikTok/Reels.

**Time investment:** 1 shooting day + 2–3 editing days.

---

### Option B: Mid-Tier Production ($2,000 — $5,000)
**Best for:** YouTube pre-roll, Facebook ads, app store assets

**What to hire:**
- Videographer with commercial reel: $800–$1,500/day
- 1 shooting day (truck, driver, road + dock)
- Professional color grade: $300–$500
- Motion graphics / titles: $400–$800
- Licensed music composition: $200–$500
- Voice-over (if used): $100–$300

**Realistic output:** 30s promo (all formats), 15s cut, B-roll library for future use.

**Timeline:** 1 shoot day → 1–2 weeks post-production.

---

### Option C: Full Production ($10,000 — $25,000)
**Best for:** National launch campaign, TV spots, XR experience

**What's included:**
- Director + DP team
- 2–3 shooting days (multiple locations)
- Actor drivers or real owner-operator casting
- Full location permits (highway, loading dock, truck stop)
- Professional color + VFX
- Full spatial/XR video production (separate specialist)
- Multi-platform delivery package
- Reshoots included

**Realistic output:** All deliverables above + XR spatial video + B-roll library (100+ clips) + director's cut.

## RECOMMENDED PRODUCTION APPROACH

**Phase 1 (now):** DIY — film with a real driver, focus on authenticity. Post on TikTok and Reels. Test which message resonates.

**Phase 2 (after initial traction):** Mid-tier production for YouTube and Facebook ad campaigns. Use real testimonials if possible.

**Phase 3 (scale):** Full production + XR experience for App Store / Vision Pro launch.

---

## CASTING NOTES

If using actors:
- Cast real truck drivers or people who look like them (weathered, strong, practical)
- Ages 30–50 preferred
- Avoid: young "tech influencer" types, excessive grooming, obviously non-drivers
- Diversity: include at least one Black or Hispanic driver (both demographics are significant in US trucking)

If using real users:
- Better authenticity, but harder to control quality
- Film 2–3 drivers, use the best footage
- Offer free premium subscription as compensation
